Information

Teaching English in China is a great opportunity for those who would like to gain experience teaching, while having the chance to explore a country and a part of the world that is home to some the oldest and most interesting civilizations. China has countless beautiful attractions, some of the world’s most historic landmarks and a rich culture embedded into so many aspects of the the Chinese lifestyle.

School schedules in China are similar to other countries, however, during summer holidays and winter breaks, our teachers have the opportunities to travel, not only to different parts of China, but also to parts of Asia that would typically be exceedingly costly travelling from other parts of the world.

Countries like Thailand, Malaysia, Japan, Korea, Singapore, Philippines, Vietnam, Bali and India, just to name a few, are only a short plane ride from China, and tickets, in most cases, are more affordable than flying domestically within most western countries.

What Can You Expect ... The Process

Simple, Easy & Safe

Register to become a teacher by contacting us directly and providing some basic information about yourself.

A Skype call or in person interview is set up to discuss the opportunity and your current skill sets.

Confirm your position by signing off on contracts and obtaining your entry visa passport.

Hop on a plane and come to China where a representative will greet you at the airport and escort you to your accommodations.
